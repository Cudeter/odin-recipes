Cucumber Salad

Sunny Days Beckon This Easy Cucumber Salad

There's so much to love about this recipe. It's easy to prepare, uses the most simplest ingredients

Its filled with fresh flavours.

You've got crisp cucumber and onions,lots of dill and a splash of tangy -sweet dressing

Ingredients;

  • 2 large English cucumbers (thinly sliced)
  • 1 tablespoon of kosher salt
  • Â½ red onion (thinly sliced)
  • 2 green onions (thinly sliced)
  • 3 tablespoons of fresh dill (chopped)

Steps

  1. Slice the cucumber and the onions
  2. Toss cucumbers with salt
  3. Wash and drain the cucumbers
  4. Stir together the dressing ingredients in a small bowl
  5. Add the cucumbers,sliced red and green onion,and dill to a large serving bowl
